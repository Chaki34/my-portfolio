Beneath the crashing waves of uncertainty, I found a profound stillness. Navigating through the deepest blue of my own thoughts, I realized that storms only exist on the surface. Deep down, where the pressure is greatest, lies the anchor of true clarity and the quiet strength to weather any gale.
